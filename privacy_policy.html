Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

Expense Tracker (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This document explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

1. Information We Access

  • SMS (Optional): If you choose to grant SMS permission, we read bank transaction messages only to identify your income and expenses automatically.
  • We never read or store personal messages, OTPs, or promotional SMS.

2. How Your Data Is Used

  • SMS data is analyzed locally on your device to detect transaction amounts, merchants, and dates.
  • No SMS content or personal data ever leaves your phone or is shared with anyone.
  • We do not run ads or use analytics SDKs that collect personal information.

3. Optional Permission & Revocation

  • Granting SMS access is entirely optional. You may continue using manual expense entry without granting it.
  • You can revoke permission anytime in your device’s system settings under “App → Permissions.”

4. Data Storage

All expense data is stored securely in your device’s local storage or private app database. We do not upload or transmit it to our servers.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process SMS information solely based on your explicit consent when you grant the SMS permission within the app.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third party.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy
